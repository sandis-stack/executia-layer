§ 03 — Verification Logic

Execution verification logic.

01
Authorization
The requesting authority holds the mandate to execute the specified action within defined scope.
02
Compliance
Institutional policy, regulatory constraints, and budget parameters are enforced at execution time — not post-audit.
03
Responsibility
A confirmed, accountable actor is assigned before execution proceeds. Responsibility cannot be dissolved after the fact.
04
Resource availability
Required resources, budget envelope, and operational conditions are confirmed prior to execution signal emission.

All conditions must be satisfied simultaneously. Otherwise, execution is structurally blocked — not flagged, not logged.

All conditions verified → Execution signal emitted
Any condition fails → Execution does not occur
